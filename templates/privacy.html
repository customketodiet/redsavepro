Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

This is a general-purpose template, not legal advice. If you serve users in the EU (GDPR) or California (CCPA), have this reviewed by a privacy professional before publishing — those laws have specific disclosure requirements not fully covered here.

1. What we don't collect

RedSave Pro does not require an account, email address, or sign-up. We do not store the Reddit URLs you submit, the videos or audio you download, or any file content. Video and audio are streamed directly from Reddit's CDN to your device and are not saved on our server.

2. What may be temporarily logged

Like virtually any web server, our hosting infrastructure may briefly retain standard technical logs — IP address, timestamp, and browser user-agent — for security monitoring and abuse prevention. These logs are not used to build a profile of you, are not sold, and are not linked to the content you download.

3. Third parties

To do its job, RedSave Pro contacts Reddit's servers (reddit.com and v.redd.it) on your behalf to fetch the video you requested. That request is subject to Reddit's own Privacy Policy. Our hosting provider may also process connection-level data as part of running the server infrastructure.

4. Children's privacy

RedSave Pro is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Your choices

Because no account or persistent personal data is stored, there is no profile to access, export, or delete. If you believe we hold information about you and want it removed, contact us using the details below.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the Service changes. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision.
